Forward Kinematics (FK)
Pro: Primary means of rigging, easiest, quickest, most stable, can be used in about any circumstance. 
Con: It can be a bit clunky, hard to animate.
FK works on the principle of Hierarchy

Importance of Hierarchy 
Parent/Child relationship

Torso
 > Head
 > Hips
   > Legs
   > Feet
     > Digits
 > Arms
   > Hands
     > Digits
